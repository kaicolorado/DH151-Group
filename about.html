Overview

We are the cARTographers, a duo of mapping students looking to research the effects that artistic education has on the American education system. Our project examines artistic education policies and standardized test scores to illuminate correlations between these two. We hope to show that exposing students to arts in education and supporting creativity has many benefits that most wouldn't think of.

How to Use the Map

Although the map starts off empty, you can select any layers from the top right to overlay data. For now, you can view whether a state has implemented any of the ten art policies. You can even select multiple layers at a time! The legend at the bottom right helps you keep track of which layers you're currently viewing.
